The ASCR community is heartbroken by the tragic loss of Greg Biffle and his family in a plane accident yesterday.

To honor his legacy, league drivers organized a special formation lap in NASCAR 25, lining up behind a replica of Greg's car to pay our respects. Our thoughts are with their friends and loved ones during this difficult time. Godspeed, #16.
