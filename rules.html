SPORTING CODE

Official ASCRL Regulations & Procedures

1.0 General Conduct

+

All drivers are expected to maintain a professional atmosphere. The ASCRL is a competitive environment, but respect for fellow competitors is mandatory.

  • No intentional "dumping" or wrecking of other competitors.
  • Verbal abuse in Discord or in-game chat will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Drivers must have a stable internet connection to ensure the safety of the field.

2.0 Race Weekend Format

+

Standard race nights follow a strict timeline to ensure broadcast quality and driver convenience.

Timeline (EST)

  • 8:00 PM: Open Practice / Server Launch
  • 9:00 PM: Mandatory Driver Briefing & Qualifying
  • 9:20 PM: Green Flag / Race Start

*Failure to attend the driver briefing may result in a back-of-the-field penalty.
