Official ASCRL Regulations

SPORTING CODE

1.0 General Conduct

+

All drivers are expected to maintain a professional atmosphere. The ASCRL is a competitive environment, but respect for fellow competitors is mandatory.

  • No intentional "dumping" or wrecking of other competitors.
  • Verbal abuse in Discord or in-game chat will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Drivers must have a stable internet connection to ensure the safety of the field.
  • Drivers must use a wheel and pedal set. No controllers or keyboards permitted.
  • Paint schemes must not contain offensive language or imagery.

Any violation of these conduct rules will be reviewed by the stewardship committee and may result in point deductions or race suspensions.

2.0 Race Weekend Format

+

Standard race nights follow a strict timeline to ensure broadcast quality and driver convenience. Times listed are Eastern Standard Time (EST).

Timeline (EST)

  • 8:00 PM: Open Practice / Server Launch
  • 9:00 PM: Qualifying (Group Qualifying, 3% Race)
  • 9:10 PM: Mandatory Driver Briefing
  • 9:20 PM: Green Flag / Race Start

Restarts: Initial start occurs in the restart zone. All subsequent restarts are double-file. The leader has lane choice.

*Failure to attend the driver briefing may result in a back-of-the-field penalty.
